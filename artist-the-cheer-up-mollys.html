ARTIST

The Cheer Up Mollys

The Cheer Up Mollys are a five-piece, all-female folk and acoustic band made up of long-time friends and musical collaborators from Sussex.

"

As a band of five we love OnSong as a creative tool. It’s easy to share songs with each other, either covers or originals, using Dropbox, AirDrop or email. When we are rehearsing a new song and find the key is too high or too low for the harmonies it’s so easy to just change all the music in one go with the ‘change key’ function. The other great thing is that we can each have all the songs for rehearsal right there in front of us on our iPads without having to search through piles of paper to find the one we want to play.
